Here's all information about Freecodecamp.org

  • 2014 - freeCodeCamp was launched in October 2014 and incorporated as Free Code Camp, Inc.
  • 2015 - freeCodeCamp introduced its first full-stack developer certification.
  • 2016 - The platform reached over 100,000 users worldwide.
  • 2017 - freeCodeCamp's curriculum was expanded to include six certifications, covering web development and data visualization.
  • 2018 - The freeCodeCamp YouTube channel was launched to provide video tutorials and interviews.
  • 2018 - freeCodeCamp updated its curriculum to include Python, machine learning, and coding interview preparation.
  • 2019 - The platform surpassed 40,000 graduates with verified certifications.
  • 2019 - freeCodeCamp launched a publications platform for articles on programming and technology.
  • 2020 - During the COVID-19 pandemic, freeCodeCamp introduced new tools and free resources to help students learn remotely.
  • 2021 - The curriculum was updated with 10 new certifications, including Responsive Web Design and Scientific Computing with Python.
  • 2021 - freeCodeCamp introduced "Coding Interview Prep" to help developers prepare for technical interviews.
  • 2022 - The platform released new tutorials on emerging technologies, including blockchain and cloud computing.
  • 2022 - freeCodeCamp expanded its community with over 7,000 local study groups worldwide.
  • 2023 - freeCodeCamp introduced Spanish, Chinese, and other language support to make coding education accessible globally.
  • 2023 - The platform's YouTube channel surpassed 5 million subscribers and became one of the largest coding education channels online.
